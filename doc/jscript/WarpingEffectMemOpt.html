warpingEffect

WarpingEffectEnum warpingEffect()

Description:
*Warping Effect* Warping effect according to theory description and expressions given in: AISC, Steel Design Guide Series 9, Torsional Analysis of Structural Steel Members, Seaburg and Carter, 1997 (2nd print 2003). Implemented for double symmetric I/H shaped profiles and channel profiles.Maximum torsional moment along the member is regarded as the design torsional moment to be used in connection with the selected warping calculation method (Case 3, 4 or 7).
