Command: ReparameterizedBeamCurveOffset

Description:
Modifies the distribution of the eccentricity of this beam along the axial direction: Eccentric joints will move beam eccentricity of the intersection to the eccentric intersection. Structural intersections will move beam eccentricity of the intersection to the eccentric intersection. Segment boundaries will not be locked along the axial direction of the eccentric beam.
