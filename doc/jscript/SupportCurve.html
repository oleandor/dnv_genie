Command: SupportCurve

Description:
Represent a Support Curve
Example of use:
Insert support conditions along a line; curved or straight.
Sc5 = SupportCurve(ModelCurve(Point(24 m,5 m,0 m), Point(24.5 m,5 m,0 m), Point(25 m,5.5 m,0 m)));
Sc5.localSystemRule = ConstantLocalSystem(LocalSystem(Vector3d(1 m,0 m,0 m), Vector3d(0 m,0 m,1 m)));
Sc5.boundary = BoundaryCondition(Fixed, Free, Free, Free, Fixed, Fixed);
This command is scripted. Typically, when defining a support curve SC5 and modify some of the degrees of freedom
