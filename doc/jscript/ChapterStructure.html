Overview Class Hierarchy  GeniE V8.5-04 25-Jan-2023

Command: ChapterStructure

Description:
Document structural parts. For more information, please read to GeniE Volume 6
Example of use:
ChapterStructure
a1 = Report("a");
a1.add(ChapterStructure());
a1.element(1).setLoopSets(true);
a1.element(1).setSets(Array(bottom_deck));
a1.element(1).add(TableBeamCoordinate());
a1.element(1).add(TableBeamProperty());
a1.element(1).add(TableBeamBucklingData());
a1.element(1).add(TableBeamHydroProperty());
This command is scripted and the scripting is dependent on your choice for which parts
to include in the report. Typical parts of it may be as the example above.
