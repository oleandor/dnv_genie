Command: MaterialShear

Description:
Linear isotropic shear material
Example of use:
Create/Edit ShearMaterial
MyShearMat = MaterialShear(75, 7.85, 210000000, 0.3, 1.2e-5, 3.0e-5);
This command is scripted. Typically for an isotropic shear material named
MyShearMat with axial reduction of 75
