Command: CurveEndOffset

Description:
Reparameterizes the eccentric line so that any point on the centric line will be projected onto the eccentric line (shortest distance).The start and end eccentricities of the beam can be preserved by setting the keepAxialEccentricity* flags.This will lead to a discontinuous parameterization, where some parameter ranges on the centric beam may become invalid, restricting how fine mesh you can create on your beams.
