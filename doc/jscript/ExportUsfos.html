Command: ExportUsfos

Description:
Export GeniE model to Usfos formatted file(s)
Example of use:
Export GeniE model
UsfosExporter = ExportUsfos();
UsfosExporter.SetAnalysis(Analysis1);
UsfosExporter.DoExport("C:/Location/Location");
