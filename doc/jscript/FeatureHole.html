Command: FeatureHole

Description:
To create a hole on the assembly from a planar closed guiding curve
Example of use:
Create Hole From Guiding Curve
Hole1 = FeatureHole(guidingCurve);
This command is scripted.

CreateHoleFromProfile(ProfileRR profileCurve, Point anchorPoint, LengthVector3d normalVector, Angle rotationAngle)

Description:
Creates a hole from a rounded rectangle 2d-profile curve.
Example of use:
Create Hole From Profile
Hole1 = CreateHoleFromProfile(profileCurve,anchorPoint,normalVector,rotationAngle,translationVector);
AchorPoint is the point of the model, where the 2d-profile-center will be placed on. The profile center is the cog of its control polygon.
NormalVector is the normal vector at the anchor-point.
RotationAngle is the angle of rotation of the 2d-profile around the normal vector.
TranslationVector is a vector of 2d-translation of the 2d-profile
